(Intro to Data Science: COVID-19 Infection Statistics) During the first 20 days of
the COVID-19 pandemic, the number of newly infected patients per day in a country
were recorded. Place the following numbers in a list:

174, 335, 278, 214, 422, 513, 737, 672, 489, 412, 1301, 1105, 1123,
1376, 1502, 894, 665, 1704, 1656, 1342

Use the built-in functions in the statistics module to display the following statistics
concerning the infection rate: minimum, maximum, range, mean, median, variance, and
standard deviation.
